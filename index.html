Halloween Horror Nights Information

The World's Premier Halloween Event returns for its 35th year as the biggest names in horror join legendary icons to summon new nightmares. Don't miss Universal Orlando Halloween Horror Nights, featuring 10 haunted houses, terrifying scare zones, bold live entertainment and more.

Event Dates and Times

Universal Orlando's Halloween Horror Nights runs on select nights between August 28 - November 1 (check calendar below for all dates). The event will always start at 6:30pm and run until 2am.





Ticket Information

Univeral Orlando's Halloween Horror Nights is a seperately ticketed event outside of regular admission to the park. There are multiple ticket options to choose from including single night tickets and packages. Below is more information about these tickets and their prices.

  • Single Night Ticket - Starting from $88.99
  • Scream Early Ticket - Starting from $70.00
  • Express Pass - Starting from $199.99

To purchase tickets, please visit: Universal Orlando Tickets





Event Offerings

There are many different scary experiences to be had at Halloween Horror Nights. The offerings for this event include:

  • 10 Terrifying Haunted Houses
  • 5 Sinister Scare Zones
  • Outrageous Live Entertainment
  • Exhilarating Rides and Attractions
  • Themed Food and Drinks
  • Themed Merchandise
